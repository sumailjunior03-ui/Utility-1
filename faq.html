FAQ

What counts as a business day?

A business day is Monday through Friday, excluding U.S. federal holidays. Weekends are never counted.

Are U.S. federal holidays included?

Yes. Observed U.S. federal holidays are excluded from business day counts.

What does "Include start date as Day 1" mean?

If enabled, the start date is counted as Day 1 only if it is a business day.

How does "Days Between Dates" work?

This mode counts business days between two dates, excluding weekends and holidays.

Do you store my data?

No. Calculations run in your browser and inputs are not stored.
